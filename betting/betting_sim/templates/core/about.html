About Betting Simulation Platform

Overview

The Betting Simulation Platform is a powerful tool for designing, running, and analyzing betting simulations. It allows you to model various betting scenarios with configurable parameters and strategies to understand long-term outcomes and optimize your approach.

Key Features

  • Configurable Parameters: Set your starting bankroll, number of rounds, and define custom outcomes with specific probabilities and multipliers.
  • Multiple Strategies: Choose from built-in strategies like Fixed Fraction, Kelly Criterion, and Martingale, or create and upload your own custom strategy.
  • Parameter Sweeping: Run simulations across a range of parameter values to identify optimal configurations.
  • Rich Visualizations: Analyze simulation results with interactive charts and graphs.
  • Save and Compare: Save your simulation configurations and results for later analysis and comparison.

Built-in Strategies

Strategy Description
Fixed Fraction Bet a fixed percentage of your current bankroll on each round. Simple but effective for managing risk.
Kelly Criterion Optimizes the bet size to maximize the expected logarithm of wealth. Mathematically optimal under certain conditions.
Martingale Double your bet after each loss to recover previous losses plus a small profit. High risk with potential for catastrophic ruin.
Custom Strategy Upload your own Python script with a bet_fraction function to implement any custom betting strategy.
